# Starcloud Funding, Revenue, and OpenAI Orbital Data Center Interest

**Multiple sources aggregated**

**Date**: 2025–2026

## Starcloud
- Raised $170M in funding as of latest round
- Valuation: ~$1.1B
- 18 customers signed up on Kepler Communications network
- Two business models: (1) on-orbit edge compute for other spacecraft, (2) eventual terrestrial cloud competition
- 16U cubesat form factor (initial satellite)
- Uses Kepler Communications network as backhaul
- No public revenue figures disclosed; likely pre-revenue based on public announcements

## Sam Altman / OpenAI and Orbital Data Centers
- Sam Altman reportedly looked into investing in Stoke Space for orbital data center capabilities (GeekWire, December 2025)
- Talks reportedly no longer active as of early 2026
- Notion of OpenAI building their own orbital data centers was based on Altman's personal exploration; no formal OpenAI corporate initiative confirmed
- Anthropic actively pursuing orbital compute partnerships (separate from OpenAI)
