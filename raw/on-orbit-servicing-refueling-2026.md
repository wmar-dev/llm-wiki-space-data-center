# On-Orbit Servicing and Refueling — 2026 Market Analysis

Source: New Space Economy (newspaceeconomy.ca), March 31, 2026
Type: Industry analysis
Date retrieved: 2026-06-07

Key findings:
- Northrop Grumman MEV-1/2 already demonstrated commercial GEO life extension (docked Feb 2020, Apr 2021)
- MEV-1 demonstrated first-ever undocking between commercial spacecraft in GEO (Apr 2025)
- Astroscale U.S. Provisioner refueler manifested for 2026 launch, first refueling of Space Force asset
- Orbit Fab developing fueling ports (RAFTI) and depots; two refueling payloads launching 2026
- NASA OSAM-1 cancelled due to cost/schedule and shift away from unprepared spacecraft refueling
- DARPA RSGS program advancing robotic servicing in GEO
- MRV (Mission Robotic Vehicle) + MEP (Mission Extension Pods) on track for 2026 launch
- Market moving toward "prepared client" satellites with standard interfaces
- GEO life extension is demonstrated; broad LEO servicing still faces replacement-cost economics
