# xAI Colossus Supercomputer — NADDOD Analysis

**URL**: https://naddod.com/blog/why-xai-is-building-the-worlds-fastest-supercomputer-colossus
**Date**: 2025
**Source**: NADDOD
**Type**: industry_report

Analysis of xAI's Colossus supercomputer in Memphis, Tennessee.

Key details:
- Initially launched with 100,000 NVIDIA H100 GPUs
- Expanded to approximately 450,000+ GPUs by December 2025 (mix including H200s)
- Phase 2 expansion targeting ~1 million GPUs
- Total cluster peak power: ~200 MW (initial)
- 1 GW expansion planned for next phase
- Built in a repurposed furniture factory in Memphis
- Connected via a 15-mile fiber loop
- Gas turbines power the facility
- Built on "never-been-done-before timescales" (~122 days to initial deployment)
- Elite data center developer CyrusOne involved in development
- Model mix: H100s initially, H200s added in Phase 1B, GB200 NVL72 racks planned for next expansion phase
- Each NVL72 rack: 72 GPUs per rack
- Phase 2 targeting ~1 GW with advanced cooling and power infrastructure
