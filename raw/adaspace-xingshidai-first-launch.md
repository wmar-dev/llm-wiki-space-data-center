# AdaSpace launches first 12 satellites for LEO constellation Xingshidai

**URL**: https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/adaspace-launches-first-12-satellites-for-leo-constellation-xingshidai/
**Date**: 2025
**Source**: Data Center Dynamics (DCD)
**Type**: news_article

AdaSpace (Adaspace) has launched the first 12 satellites of its planned 2,800-satellite constellation called Xingshidai (Xingshidai Project). Each satellite is capable of performing AI inferencing in orbit, running an 8 billion parameter AI model. The satellites use 100 Gbps optical inter-satellite laser communication links.

Key details:
- Beijing-based AdaSpace founded in 2020
- Project aims to create a "Global Space AI Computing Network"
- Satellites use the XingShengLi-100 satellite bus platform
- Constellation construction started end of 2023
- Each satellite performs on-orbit AI inference
- 100 Gbps optical inter-satellite links
- Second batch of 12 units planned for subsequent launch
- Positioned as indigenous Chinese development analogous to DeepSeek
