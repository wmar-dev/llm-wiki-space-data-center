# FAA Streamlines Commercial Space License Approvals — Part 450

Source: FAA press release, March 17, 2026
Type: Government source
Date retrieved: 2026-06-07

Key findings:
- All commercial space licensing now under Part 450 rule (consolidates 4 old rules)
- Part 450 allows one license for portfolio of operations, configurations, and sites
- FAA reached 1,000 licensed space operations by August 14, 2025
- 180-day statutory timeline for license processing; 120 days for permits
- 14 Part 450 licenses issued since March 2021
- Major operators transitioned by March 9, 2026 deadline (SpaceX, Blue Origin, ULA, Rocket Lab, Firefly)
