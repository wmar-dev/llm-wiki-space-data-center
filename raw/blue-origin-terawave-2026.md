# Blue Origin TeraWave 2026 — Communications Constellation

Source: Via Satellite, Blue Origin press release, FCC filing (Jan 21, 2026)
Type: Industry report / news article
Date retrieved: 2026-06-07

Blue Origin announced TeraWave, a 5,408-satellite constellation (5,280 LEO + 128 MEO) for enterprise data center and government connectivity. Uses Q/V-band RF (up to 144 Gbps per customer) and optical links (up to 6 Tbps). Deployment starts Q4 2027.

Positioned as communications infrastructure, not orbital compute. FCC filing calls it "logical progression" into advanced communications. Supports up to ~100,000 customers. Competes with Amazon Leo for enterprise connectivity market.
