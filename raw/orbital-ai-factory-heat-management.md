# Orbital AI Factory — Heat Management & Radiators

Source: Orbital AI Factory website (orbitalaifactory.com)
Type: Industry report
Date retrieved: 2026-06-07

Orbital AI Factory publishes radiator design for megawatt-class orbital data center:
- 2-4 deployable radiator assemblies per node, ISS-derived scissors-beam mechanism
- Per assembly: ~22-23 m × 6-7 m = ~140-160 m²
- Total: ~1,700-2,000 m² effective radiator area for 1 MW thermal load
- Ammonia coolant loop; gimbaled panels for sun avoidance
- High-emissivity IR coating, low visible/near-IR absorptivity
- Design targets high area-to-mass ratio for cost-effective launch
