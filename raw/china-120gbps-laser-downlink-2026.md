# China Hits 120 Gbps in Satellite-to-Ground Laser Communications

Source: CGTN (China Global Television Network), January 30, 2026

Source type: news article

---

## Key Facts

- **Peak data rate:** 120 Gbps (with data rates "exceeding 100 Gbps" sustained)
- **Satellite:** AIRSAT-02
- **Ground station:** Pamir Plateau, Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region
  - China's first satellite-to-ground laser communication station to operate in commercialized, routine mode (operational since September 2024)
  - Aperture: 500 mm laser communication system
- **Contact window:** Maximum continuous communication duration of 108 seconds per pass
- **Link acquisition:** Second-level acquisition speed; >93% success rate
- **Data per pass:** 12.656 terabits per 108-second window (= ~1.58 TB)
- **Altitude:** Not specified in article

## Historical Context
- 2023: 10 Gbps breakthrough
- 2025: 60 Gbps
- January 2026: 120 Gbps (doubling from previous record)
