# Orbital

**Type**: Company (venture-funded)
**CEO**: Euwyn Poon (formerly CEO of Spin electric scooters)
**Funding**: a16z speedrun program
**Sector**: Space data centers / orbital compute

## Overview

Orbital is a venture-backed startup developing commercial orbital data centers. Funded by a16z, the company plans to deploy satellites equipped with AI inference hardware in Low Earth Orbit.

## Current Status (as of April 2026)

- First proof-of-concept flight targeting 2027
- Full-scale satellite targeting 2030
- Planned chip: Nvidia Space-1 Vera Rubin module

## Satellite Design

- Power capacity: **100 kW per satellite** — CEO describes this as "the sweet spot"
- Solar array: ~two halves of a tennis court
- Radiator: ~half the solar array area
- Physical size: ~four refrigerators

## CEO Position on Economics

Euwyn Poon has publicly acknowledged that "the economics of launch don't quite work yet" as of April 2026, requiring a reduction from ~$7,000/kg to ~$10/kg ("a couple orders of magnitude"). — [[wiki/sources/orbital-startup-economics-register-2026.md]] *(news_article)*
