# Aetherflux / Cowboy Space Corporation

**Type**: Company (venture-funded)
**Also known as**: Cowboy Space Corporation (renamed from Aetherflux)
**Funding**: $50 million
**Sector**: Space infrastructure / space data centers

## Overview

Originally known as Aetherflux, this venture-funded startup proposed a constellation
of small LEO satellites using infrared lasers to beam solar power to Earth (ground
stations 5–10 meters in diameter). The company received support from the US
Department of Defense's Operational Energy Capability Improvement Fund.

In December 2025, the company pivoted from space-based solar power to
**space-based data centers** [[wiki/sources/space-based-solar-power.md]] *(other)*
and subsequently renamed itself **Cowboy Space Corporation**
[[wiki/sources/space-data-center-wikipedia.md]] *(other)*.

## Significance

The pivot and rename signal that LEO laser infrastructure originally developed for
SBSP is being repurposed for data center applications. The underlying architecture —
small satellites, infrared laser links, small ground stations — transfers directly
to orbital compute.
