# LEO ISL Handoff and Latency Research

Synthesis of academic research (2025-2026) on LEO ISL latency: end-to-end delay dominated by propagation delay (~5-25 ms at 550 km); ISL routing achieves 20-30 ms total; handoff penalty <10 ms; ISL optimization reduces max delay by 3-9 ms. Laser ISLs offer >1 Gbps.
