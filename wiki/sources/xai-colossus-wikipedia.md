# xAI Colossus Datacenter (Wikipedia)

**Source**: Wikipedia — xAI (company) article
**Source type**: other
**Processing status**: processed
**Domain relevance**: supporting (terrestrial hyperscale AI compute baseline for space data center comparison)

## Key Claims

- **Location**: Memphis, Tennessee — operational December 2024 (122-day buildout). — *(other)*
- **Peak power (current)**: **150 MW** at peak. — *(other)*
- **Estimated full power**: ~300 MW (solar expansion adds 30 MW described as "10% of estimated power use"). — *(other)*
- **GPU expansion target**: Plans announced to expand to **at least 1 million GPUs**. — *(other)*
- **Compute expansion (Dec 30, 2025)**: Third building purchased; target "nearly **2 gigawatts** of compute power." — *(other)*
- **Power supply**: Initially 14 VoltaGrid portable methane-gas generators; by May 2025 at least 33 generators observed via thermal imaging. — *(other)*
- **Generator scale**: Portable generators "generate about the same amount of power as the Tennessee Valley Authority's large gas-fired power plant nearby." — *(other)*
