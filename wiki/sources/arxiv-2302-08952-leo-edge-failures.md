# Edge Computing in Low-Earth Orbit — What Could Possibly Go Wrong?

**Authors**: Tobias Pfandzelter, David Bermbach
**Source type**: peer_reviewed (arxiv preprint)
**Origin URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.08952

## Summary

Presents a taxonomy of failure modes for LEO edge computing systems, with radiation effects on compute hardware as a named failure category. The abstract page was fetched; quantitative radiation figures cited in secondary literature (1mm Al shielding, SEU rates 10⁻³–10⁻⁴/device/day, TID limits for Snapdragon SA8155P) are from the full PDF body and not independently verified via this fetch.

## Key Claims

- Taxonomy of failures in LEO edge computing includes radiation effects on compute hardware as a distinct failure mode
- Software systems for LEO edge computing must adapt to satellite mobility and hardware failure patterns including radiation-induced faults
