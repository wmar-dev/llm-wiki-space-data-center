# FAA Part 450 Licensing Streamline

FAA press release (March 17, 2026): All commercial space licensing consolidated under Part 450 rule. 14 licenses issued since 2021. FAA reached 1,000 licensed space operations by Aug 2025. 180-day statutory timeline for license processing. Major operators transitioned by March 2026 deadline.
