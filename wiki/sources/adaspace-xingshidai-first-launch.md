# AdaSpace Launches First 12 Satellites for Xingshidai LEO Constellation

**Source**: Data Center Dynamics (DCD)
**Source type**: news_article
**Processing status**: processed

## Summary

Beijing-based AdaSpace (founded 2020) has launched the first 12 satellites of its planned 2,800-satellite constellation called Xingshidai (Xingshidai Project). The constellation aims to create a "Global Space AI Computing Network" — an indigenous Chinese development analogous to DeepSeek.

## Key Claims

- **Launch**: First 12 satellites deployed; second batch of 12 planned for subsequent launch — *(news article)*
- **Constellation scale**: Planned 2,800 satellites total — *(news article)*
- **AI capability**: Each satellite runs an 8 billion parameter AI model on-orbit — *(news article)*
- **Inter-satellite links**: 100 Gbps optical laser communication links — *(news article)*
- **Bus platform**: XingShengLi-100 satellite bus — *(news article)*
- **Timeline**: Construction started end of 2023 — *(news article)*
- **Positioning**: Indigenous Chinese development, positioned alongside DeepSeek as domestic AI infrastructure — *(news article)*
