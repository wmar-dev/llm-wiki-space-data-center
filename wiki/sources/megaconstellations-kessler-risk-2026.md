# Managing Mega-Constellation Risks in LEO

Viasat analysis of mega-constellation collision risk. Key data: one-third of a single mega-constellation accounts for ~90% of close approaches; 1% failure rate on tens of thousands of satellites yields hundreds of non-maneuverable debris objects; crowding generates hundreds of millions of conjunction events. Relevance to orbital data centers: large-mass objects with reduced maneuverability disproportionately contribute to Kessler risk.
