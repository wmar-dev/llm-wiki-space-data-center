# Blue Origin TeraWave 2026

Blue Origin announced TeraWave on Jan 21, 2026: a 5,408-satellite constellation (5,280 LEO + 128 MEO) for enterprise, data center, and government connectivity. Uses Q/V-band RF (up to 144 Gbps) and optical links (up to 6 Tbps). FCC filing describes it as "advanced communications infrastructure" — a connectivity play, not orbital compute. Deployment begins Q4 2027.
